### Week 3 — Customer Intelligence System for HELP International

**Dataset:** Country Socioeconomic & Health Indicators (167 Countries)  
Covers child mortality, exports, health spending, imports, income, inflation, life expectancy, fertility rate, and GDP per capita.

**Problem Statement:**  
HELP International raised $10 million and needs to allocate it strategically. As a Data Scientist, the goal is to categorise countries using socio-economic and health factors and identify which nations are in the direst need of humanitarian aid.

**Key Work:**
- Full exploratory data analysis across 9 socioeconomic features
- Feature scaling and PCA dimensionality reduction
- K-Means clustering with elbow and silhouette score optimization (K=3)
- DBSCAN clustering for density-based segmentation and outlier detection
- Random Forest classification — 100% accuracy, 95.78% CV score
- XGBoost classification — 97.62% accuracy, 95.79% CV score
- Composite aid priority scoring to rank top 10 countries
- Actionable CEO recommendations for $10M fund allocation

**Segments Identified:**

| Segment | Countries | Key Characteristics |
|---------|-----------|---------------------|
| 🔴 Needs Aid | 47 | High child mortality, low GDP, low life expectancy |
| 🟡 Developing | 84 | Moderate indicators, transitioning economies |
| 🟢 Developed | 36 | Low child mortality, high GDP, high life expectancy |

**Top 5 Countries Requiring Aid:**
1. 🇭🇹 Haiti — Child mortality 208/1000, GDP $662
2. 🇸🇱 Sierra Leone — Child mortality 160/1000, GDP $399
3. 🇨🇫 Central African Republic — Child mortality 149/1000, GDP $446
4. 🇹🇩 Chad — Child mortality 150/1000, GDP $897
5. 🇲🇱 Mali — Child mortality 137/1000, GDP $708

**Tools:** Pandas, Scikit-learn, XGBoost, Matplotlib, Seaborn
